About Anand Bankar's GeetRatna Project

Founded in 2015, GeetRatna is dedicated to preserving authentic Marathi Christian hymns through:

  • Lyric Documentation - Original Marathi with diacritical marks
  • Faithful Translations - English and Hindi while preserving poetic beauty
  • Historical Research - Composer biographies and hymn origins
  • Devotional Resources - Biblical references and commentary
  • Audio Preservation - Traditional renditions on YouTube

This non-commercial project has documented over 100 hymns to date, with new additions monthly.
